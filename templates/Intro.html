Rainbow Button Initiative

[Go Back]

The Rainbow Button provides a set of tools to enable consumers to manage their health data.

  • Blue Button: Download your health data
  • Green Button: Allow your anonymous data to be donated to organizations of your choosing
  • White Button: Send your data to another person or entity, e.g. A specialist you will be seeing
  • Red Button: Lock part of your Health Record.

RainbowButton.com implements the GreenButton and uses the RedButton to help you de-personalize your BlueButton health file.

 

To find out more about the Rainbow Button Initiative check out our Background page.
